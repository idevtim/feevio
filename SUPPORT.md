# Support & FAQ

## Get Help

If you have a question, found a bug, or want to request a feature, [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/idevtim/feevio/issues).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What calculators are included?

Feevio includes five calculators built for small business sellers:

1. **Reverse Tax** — Find the pre-tax price from a tax-inclusive total
2. **Margin vs Markup** — See profit margin and markup side by side
3. **Break-Even** — Calculate how many units you need to sell to cover costs
4. **Marketplace Fee** — Compute platform fees and your net amount
5. **Net Payout** — Get your true profit after costs, shipping, and fees

### Does Feevio collect my data?

No. Feevio works entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for details.

### What devices does Feevio support?

Feevio requires iOS 17.0 or later.

### Do my inputs get saved?

Yes — your calculator inputs persist between sessions using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device.
